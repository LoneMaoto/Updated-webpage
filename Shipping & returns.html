Shipping & Returns

Shipping Policy

We unfortunately do not offer shipping as we are still a very new store. Please strongly consider visiting our 3 Gaborone locations which are Game City mall, Riverwalk mall and airport junction mall.

Our products do have a return policy only if the product was not directly used.

Return Policy

If you’re not 100% satisfied with your purchase, you can return your item within 30 days after purchasing, after which we do not accept returns. Returned items must be in their original condition and packaging.

Please note that returns are to be made within 30 days after which they will all be rejected even if the product is unused.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, a credit will automatically be applied to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.
